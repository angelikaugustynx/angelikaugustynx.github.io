Case Study

The Dark Room Reveal

Walk a buyer into this room and there's nothing else to look at. One light source, one red line, one car — by the time they reach it, the car has already started selling itself.

[PLACEHOLDER: Dark Room Reveal — hero render, 1920×1080]

The brief: reveal a new Porsche 911 Carrera in a way that gets a buyer to stop, stay, and remember it. The answer wasn't more — it was less. A single ceiling softbox, wall panels dark enough to disappear, and one red light line borrowed from the car's own rear light bar. Nothing competes with the car, so nothing distracts the buyer from it.

Vehicle
Porsche 911 Carrera, finished in red
Goal
Hold a buyer's full attention on one car
Walls
Fluted panels, dark grey, low-reflective
Flooring
Black, high-gloss — mirrors the car back at the buyer
Lighting
Rectangular ceiling softbox, 4-point LED detail echoing the Porsche headlight signature
Accent
Single thin red light line, referencing the car's rear light bar
